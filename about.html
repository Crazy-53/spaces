Our Story

COSMIC SPACES is the world's leading company in selling and registering space property. Since 2008, we've helped thousands of clients worldwide own a piece of the infinite universe.

What started as a dream to democratize space ownership has grown into a global movement. We've registered over 50,000 celestial properties across hundreds of galaxies, making space accessible to everyone.

We believe that space is the future, and every human deserves to own a piece of this mysterious, boundless, celestial beauty. Our mission is to bridge the gap between Earth and the cosmos, one property at a time.
